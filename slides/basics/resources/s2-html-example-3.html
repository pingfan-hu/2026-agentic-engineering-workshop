About the Film

Apollo 13 dramatizes the real 1970 mission that was meant to land on the Moon but turned into a desperate fight to bring three astronauts safely home after an oxygen tank exploded in space.

Tom Hanks stars as commander Jim Lovell, anchoring a tense, true-life story of ingenuity under pressure that became one of the most acclaimed films of the year.
