About the Film

Cast Away tells the story of a FedEx executive whose plane crashes over the Pacific, leaving him stranded alone on a deserted island for years with little more than his wits and a volleyball named Wilson for company.

Tom Hanks carries most of the film almost entirely on his own as Chuck Noland, earning a Golden Globe and an Academy Award nomination for the role.
