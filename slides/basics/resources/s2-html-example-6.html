About the Film

Captain Phillips recounts the true 2009 hijacking of the cargo ship Maersk Alabama by Somali pirates, and the tense standoff that followed as its captain tried to protect his crew.

Tom Hanks plays Captain Richard Phillips, delivering a widely praised performance whose raw final scene became one of the most talked-about of his career.
