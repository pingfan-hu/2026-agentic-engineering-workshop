About the Film

Forrest Gump follows a kind-hearted man from Alabama who, despite a low IQ, finds himself at the center of some of the defining moments of 20th-century America, all while longing for his childhood sweetheart, Jenny.

Tom Hanks plays the title role, a performance that won him his second consecutive Academy Award for Best Actor. The film won Best Picture.
