About the Film

Set during World War II, Saving Private Ryan follows a squad of U.S. soldiers sent behind enemy lines to find and bring home a paratrooper whose three brothers have been killed in action.

Tom Hanks leads the squad as Captain John Miller in Steven Spielberg's landmark war film, remembered for its harrowing and influential D-Day opening sequence.
